About Song

Song Qingzhao Zhufu is a heavy fighter, bard, artisan, and interkingdom traveler inspired by Song Dynasty China. Her path in the SCA is built from fighting, service, cultural arts, and the belief that everyone deserves room to follow their own Dream.

Origin Story

I grew up in the SCA from about age 7 to 13, came back at 21, left again at 24, and returned at 29. When I came back as an adult, I thought I would follow the path of combat archery and feast cooking. Heavy fighting called louder.

I am still a fighter first, but I also love making cool things people have never seen or tasted before. Chinese culture, bardic work, glass engraving, pyrography, service, and community all live beside the field in my version of the Dream.

The Way of Song Strong

The field teaches honesty. Service teaches humility. Art teaches patience. Community teaches love.

I try to encourage others to do what makes them happy. This is their path, not anyone else’s. I want to make a cool thing and be okay. I want to fight hard and still keep the parts of myself that create, sing, cook, and connect.

Service

I bring the Dream to life through entourage work, heralding, event support, scrolls, largesse, and building spaces where people can fight, learn, and belong.

How I Serve

  • Entourage, court support, and event logistics
  • Organizing interkingdom practices and gatherings
  • Heralding and helping recognition happen smoothly
  • Creating scrolls, glass gifts, and meaningful largesse
  • Mentoring, encouraging, and making room for others to shine

Why It Matters

I do not do the interkingdom things for awards. I do them to bring people together who would not normally have the opportunity. The field is stronger when the community around it is stronger.
